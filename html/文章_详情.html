I'm a biology lover. Like to grow some plants at home, but also often photographed their own kind of flowers, on the Internet drying sun.

02

Slowly, more and more friends began to ask me, how to grow flowers? What flowers to take care of? According to the method that sells a flower to say how to still raise died? I take good care of every day why flowers always die? As soon as I inquired about it, I found it hard to laugh or cry -- the flower seller said: "Don't water this flower! "So he did not water a drop, and of course the flowers dried out; the so-called "meticulous care" of some people means that they put them by the dark side of the computer and water them every day. The flowers do not get out of the sun, the soil is wet and the roots rot. See, the failure of novice plants, are using "imagine this should be the case" approach, rather than with the real right way to grow flowers. I just wanted to laugh at them from the bottom of my heart, but on second thought, when I first started planting flowers, it was like this: I did not know how to change pots for earth even when I bought flowers.
